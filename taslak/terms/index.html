1. About Taslak

Taslak is an AI-assisted resume review tool. It analyzes a resume that you provide (as a PDF or DOCX file) against a target job — described by a job title, an experience level, and optionally a job description — and returns a score, category breakdown, missing keywords, and improvement suggestions.

Taslak is a writing-and-review assistant, not an employment decision tool. Scores and suggestions are AI-generated estimates intended to help you improve your resume. They are not predictions of hiring outcomes, and they are not a guarantee that you will pass any Applicant Tracking System or receive any job offer.
